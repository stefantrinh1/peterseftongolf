Who is Peter Sefton?

Professional, straight and adaptable

Golf Pro Peter Sefton is an ex-European Tour Professional, who has been playing golf for over 30 years and has been teaching for over 20 years and is brilliant at helping people get the golf bug and improve their game.

What is key to the swing, and what is the best style? Peter can help you understand the technicalities and learn to better control the direction of your swing.

Based in Pine ridge in the county of Surrey. He is a well known local resident and is an accomplised player nationally and has competeted in many professional tournaments

Experience

Peter is the Head Golf Professional and has been a PGA Professional for 30 years and given over 30,000 golf lessons. He has an HND in Golf Psychology and has coached HND Golf Studies pupils, as well as being a county coach. Peter started his profession at Worplesdon Golf Club as a qualified professional before becoming Head of the South-East Golf Academy working alongside Doug McClelland. His first Head Professional job was at Merrist Wood Golf Club in 1997 and then moved OgDowns Golf Club in Wiltshire. Whilst at Ogbourne he took on the new role of Director of Golf as well as continuing his initial position of Golf Professional. .
