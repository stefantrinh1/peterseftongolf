The Venue


Pine Ridge Golf Club Opened in 1993 in a pine forest. At 6,458 yards from the championship tees, it’s long but still accessible to golfers of all abilities. For quality and value it has been ranked as one of the country’s top 10 public courses and golfers who discover it for the first time keep coming back for more..

Also featuring a driving range with 36 bays and floodlights so you can keep going into the night. And with 21 Power Tees, your practice is made easier, more fun and definitely more productive.


Location


Located near the M3(3.2 miles) and local towns of Camberley(3.4 miles) and Frimley(2.2 miles).

Plentiful free parking for all vistors to the Golf Club.

Number 2 bus from Camberley heading towards Farnbourgh.
Entry Stop - Shalbourne Rise
Exit Stop - Silver drive
Walk south to Old Bisley Road and take the first exit at the roundabout and pine ridge is 0.5 miles
