"HIT IT STRAIGHTER - FURTHER - MORE OFTEN"

And

"HAVE MORE FUN ON THE GOLF COURSE"


Golf has been my life for over 35 years. Having competed at the highest level on the European Tour, I know only to well the thrill and enjoyment playing great golf can bring and equally the frustration of being unable to consistently realise your potential. That's exactly why I have a passion and enthusiasm to coach this amazing game, to help bring more fun and enjoyment to you on the golf course. Nothing gives me greater pleasure or satisfaction than to help people improve or to receive a text from a pupil who's; just had their best ever score, reduced their handicap, won the monthly medal or even achieved their first ever birdie!!

With my vast knowledge, experience and passion for the game I can help;

  • Single figure player - looking for those fine improvements
  • Mid handicap player - trying to break into single figures
  • High handicap player - looking for more consistency and dramatic improvements
  • Non handicap player - trying to achieve your first handicap or break the 100 barrier
  • New golfer - wishing to learn this great game for life

So whether your looking to trim shots off your handicap or new to the game, let's set some goals, put together a program of improvement and get to work..... you'll never look back !!
